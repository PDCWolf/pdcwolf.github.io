Phasmophobia GAMEMODE ROULETTE!

Click the button and see which gamemode you get! - These are all intended for professional difficulty.

Outlast

You're not allowed to use any flashlight but UV. You may only use videocameras to see in the dark.

I Said SHUT UP

You might only find the ghost by using parabolic microphones. 

Specialized

The 2 non flashlight items you pick are the only ones you "know" how to use. Don't swap them, except for objective related items like candles or incense.

It's Too Cramped!

You're not allowed to hide inside closets during hunts.

Respect Christ

Crucifixes may only be held in your hand. Don't drop them, except for inside the truck.

Full Dive

Force the sanity of the entire team to 0%. Do this before any investigative work takes place.

Till the End

Whenever a hunt takes place, you must all stick together.

Unprepared

No flashlights allowed, Use the buildings's own lights. UVs are allowed, videocameras may only be used when set down.

Let the Neighbors Sleep

No bright flashlights allowed. UV Flashlights are fine.

Expensive Equipment

You might only carry stuff in your hands. No droppoing ANYTHING on the ground.

In Solitude

Whenever a hunt takes place, players are not allowed to hide together.

Benefactor

One person, chosen at random, shall be the sole provider of equipment for the job.

Direct Comms

Do not seek evidence or talk to the ghost until you find the Ouija board. Once found, ask the ghost where it is until it answers. Reroll if no Ouija.

One Way

Once you've set foot inside, don't leave the house until investigation and objectives are done.

Are you here?

You're only allowed to find the ghost using the spirit box. Only swap it out when it's confirmed the ghost doesn't respond.

Squid Game

"Full Dive", "One Way", "In Solitude" and "It's Too Cramped" are in effect until a single person remains alive.
